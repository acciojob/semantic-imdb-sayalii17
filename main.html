Sita Ramam is the story of orphaned Indian Army Lieutenant Ram, who receives letters from a mysterious woman named Sita Mahalakshmi in 1964. Their love story unfolds through these letters across two timelines, intertwined with political conflict and a quest in 1985 by a Pakistani student, Afreen, to deliver a final unposted letter from Ram to Sita.

Main Actors

  • Dulquer Salmaan as Lieutenant Ram
  • Mrunal Thakur as Princess Noorjahan / Sita Mahalakshmi
  • Rashmika Mandanna as Afreen
